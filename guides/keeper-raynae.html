Stranger to Best Friend

Corrupted Globule

Located at Lorlathil in the center of Val'sharah, Keeper Raynae can be found standing beside the river.

While standing near her, you'll get an aura which allows you to fish up Corrupted Globule. Handing these to Ilyssia will increase your reputation with her; they're also used as currency for the items she sells.

  • Corrupted Globule give reputation each.

While fishing at this location, you also have a chance to reel in a Hatecoil Spearhead. This can be thrown back into the water to summon Hatecoil Spirit, who once defeated increases the catch-rate of Corrupted Globule to 100% for a short time.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the Hatecoil Spearhead, the faster it will go!
