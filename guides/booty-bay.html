Hated to Neutral

Southsea Pirates (Recommended)

One of the most popular methods to obtaining reputation with the four original goblin towns is to kill Southsea Pirates located east of Caverns of Time in Tanaris.

Despite being slower than the alternative method to obtaining neutral, this method does not reduce your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers, allowing you to be exalted with both the Bloodsail Buccaneers and the goblin towns at the same time.

  • Southsea Pirates award reputation per kill.
  • To reach neutral you will need to kill more pirates.

Traitor to the Bloodsail

The fastest method to recover your reputation with Booty Bay is to repeat the Traitor to the Bloodsail quest. The repeatable quest involves taking 40x Silk Cloth and 4x Red Dye to the Bloodsail Traitor located outside Booty Bay.

This method will reduce your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers. If you wish to maintain your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers, use the alternative method.

  • Traitor to the Bloodsail rewards reputation.
  • To reach neutral you will need to make hand-ins. (x Silk Cloth and x Red Dye)
