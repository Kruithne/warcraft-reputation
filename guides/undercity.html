Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Undercity:

  • Tirisfal Glades
  • Silverpine Forest
  • Western Plaguelands
  • Hillsbrad Foothills
  • The Hinterlands

Dungeons

The Undercity Tabard can be purchased from Captain Donald Adams in Undercity which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Undercity.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Undercity Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Undercity using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
