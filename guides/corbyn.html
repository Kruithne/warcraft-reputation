Stranger to Best Friend

Shiny Bauble

Located on the eastern coast of Shield's Rest in Stormheim, Corbyn can be found perched on a small rock.

While standing at the coast beside him, you'll get an aura which allows you to fish up Shiny Baubles. Handing these to Corbyn will increase your reputation with him; they're also used as currency for the items he sells.

  • Shiny Baubles give reputation each.

While fishing at this location, you also have a chance to reel in a White Sparkly Bauble. This can be thrown back into the water to summon an Ancient Vrykul Spirit, who once defeated increases the catch-rate of Shiny Bauble to 100% for a short time.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the White Sparkly Bauble, the faster it will go!
