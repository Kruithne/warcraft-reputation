Stranger to Best Friend

Fragmented Enchantment

Located just north of Illidari Stand in Azshara, Ilyssia can be found standing beside a small pond.

While standing near her, you'll get an aura which allows you to fish up Fragmented Enchantment. Handing these to Ilyssia will increase your reputation with her; they're also used as currency for the items she sells.

  • Fragmented Enchantment give reputation each.

While fishing at this location, you also have a chance to reel in a Disgusting Ooze. This can be thrown back into the water to summon Hatecoil Spirit, who once defeated increases the catch-rate of Fragmented Enchantment to 100% for a short time.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the Disgusting Ooze, the faster it will go!
