Stranger to Best Friend

Golden Minnow

Located in Suramar, Sha'leth can be found on the bridge above the aquarium in Suramar City.

While standing near her, you'll get an aura which allows you to fish up Golden Minnow. Handing these to Sha'leth will increase your reputation with her; they're also used as currency for the items she sells.

  • Golden Minnow give reputation each.

While fishing at this location, you also have a chance to reel in a Glowing Fish Scale. This can be thrown back into the water to summon Magicus, who once defeated drops a large amount of Golden Minnow.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the Glowing Fish Scale, the faster it will go!
