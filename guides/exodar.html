Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Gnomeregan:

  • Azuremyst Isle
  • Bloodmyst Isle
  • Swamp of Sorrows
  • Hellfire Peninsula
  • Zangarmarsh

Dungeons

The Exodar Tabard can be purchased from Kadu in Exodar which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Exodar.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Exodar Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Exodar using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
