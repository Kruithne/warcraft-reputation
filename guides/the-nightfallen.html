Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Suramar

The Nightfallen are the primary faction in Suramar. Completing the quests in the zone, as well as the Suramar City quest-line, will reward you with a significant amount of reputation.

World Quests: Suramar

Similar to the quests, many of the world quests found in Suramar at level 110 or higher award reputation with The Nightfallen. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary reward available for The Nightfallen, offering extra reputation.

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with The Nightfallen can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding reputation.
