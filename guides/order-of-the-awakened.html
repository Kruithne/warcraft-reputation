Neutral to Exalted

Daily: Unseen Influence (Recommended)

Dawn-Seeker Krisek can be found in Tanaan Jungle at Lion's Watch for Alliance and Vol'mar for Horde.

Once per day you can complete his daily quest Unseen Influence to collect Fel-Corrupted Apexis Fragments from around Tanaan Jungle.

The fastest way to complete this quest is to search for Radiating Apexis Shards at Farlight Terrace.

This daily rewards reputation. To reach Exalted, you'll need to complete it for more days.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
