Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with the Bilgewater Cartel:

  • Azshara
  • Tanaris
  • Stranglethorn Vale
  • Thousand Needles
  • Twilight Highlands

Dungeons

The Bilgewater Cartel Tabard can be purchased from Frizzo Villamar in Orgrimmar which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to the Bilgewater Cartel.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Bilgewater Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with the Bilgewater Cartel using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
