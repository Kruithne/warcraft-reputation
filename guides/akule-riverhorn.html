Stranger to Best Friend

Ancient Totem Fragments

Located at the foot of Thunder Totem in Highmountain, Akule Riverhorn can be found fishing out of a small boat.

While standing on this boat, you'll get an aura which allows you to fish up Ancient Totem Fragments. Handing these to Akule Riverhorn will increase your reputation with him; they're also used as currency for the items he sells.

  • Ancient Totem Fragments give reputation each.

While fishing at this location, you also have a chance to reel in a Water Totem Figurine. This can be thrown back into the water to summon Tarn Riverhorn, who once defeated increases the catch-rate of Ancient Totem Fragments to 100% for a short time.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the Water Totem Figurine, the faster it will go!
