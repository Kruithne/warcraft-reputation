Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Spires of Arak

One of the fastest ways to obtain a large portion of reputation with the Arakkoa Outcasts is to complete the associated quest-lines found in Spires of Arak.

Construct a level 3 Trading Post in your garrison first for to benefit from the 20% extra reputation Trading Pact provides.

Spires of Arak

Reputation with the Arakkoa Outcasts can be obtained by slaying enemies located in level 100 areas of Spires of Arak. One of the popular farming points is Lost Veil Anzu, located to the east of Sethekk Hollow.

The enemies here give reputation when slain. To reach Exalted, you need to slay more!

Garrison Mission: The Bloodmane Scrolls

The rare garrison mission The Bloodmane Scrolls rewards players with an account-bound Relic of Rukhmar token when completed successfully.

  • Relics of Rukhmar award reputation with Arakkoa Outcasts.
  • To reach Exalted you'll need more tokens.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
