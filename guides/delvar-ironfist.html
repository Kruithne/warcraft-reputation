Bodyguard to Personal Wingman

Bodyguard

When you have the Barracks building in your garrison, you can recruit a bodyguard to accompany you. While following you on Draenor, anything you kill will reward reputation with your active bodyguard.

One of the most popular places to farm reputation with your bodyguard is The Burning Front in Talador.

Delvar Ironfist is recruited during the introduction quests from your garrison.
