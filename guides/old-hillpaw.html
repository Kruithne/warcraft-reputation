Stranger to Best Friend

Favorite Food

Once per day, you can take Old Hillpaw 5 of his favorite food, which is Brasied Turtle, for reputation. Located in Valley of the Four Winds, Old Hillpaw will either be at the Halfhill market, or at the location marked on the map below.

To reach Best Friend using this method, you'll need to hand-in x Brasied Turtle over days.

Dark Soil

Dark Soil can be found all around Valley of the Four Winds. When looted, you'll find a rare treasure which can be handed in to any of farm friends to earn reputation with them.

Old Hillpaw prefers Blue Feathers. The reputation breakdown for him is:

  • Blue Feather rewards reputation.
  • Ruby Shard rewards reputation.
  • Lovely Apple rewards reputation.
  • Jade Cat rewards reputation.
  • Marsh Lily rewards reputation.

Timewalking: Mists of Pandaria

During the Mists of Pandaria timewalking event, these treasures can be bought from Mistweaver Xia on Timeless Isle for Timewarped Badges.

To complete this reputation, you would need more Blue Feathers, costing a total of badges.
