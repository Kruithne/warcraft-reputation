World Quests: The Wardens

World quests for The Wardens can be found all over the Broken Isles, offering reputation with the faction. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary reward available for The Wardens, offering extra reputation.

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with The Wardens can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding 1500 reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding 250 reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding 250 reputation.
