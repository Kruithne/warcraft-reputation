Neutral to Exalted

Fang'rila

Fang'rila, located south of Lion's Watch in Tanaan Jungle, is filled with elite saberon.

Each saberon slain awards reputation. To reach Exalted you'll need to slay more.

In addition to reputation, these saberon also drop Blackfang Claws which are used to buy items from the quartermaster as well as criteria for the I Came, I Clawed, I Conquered achievement.

Dailies: Tanaan Jungle

The daily quest Tooth and Claw in Tanaan Jungle is given by Nimi Brightcastle in Vol'mar for Horde players, and Parvink in Lion's Watch for Alliance players.

This daily is not available every day.

Completing the Tooth and Claw daily awards reputation. To reach Exalted, you'll need to complete this daily for more days.

In addition, players can pick-up the daily quest Rumble in the Jungle from Z'tenga the Walker near Fang'rila.

Rumble in the Jungle awards reputation. Completing this daily more times will get you to Exalted!

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
