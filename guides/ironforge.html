Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Ironforge:

  • Dun Morogh
  • Loch Modan
  • Wetlands

Dungeons

The Ironforge Tabard can be purchased from Captain Stonehelm in Ironforge which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Ironforge.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Ironforge Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Ironforge using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
