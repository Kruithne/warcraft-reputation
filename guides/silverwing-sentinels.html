Warsong Gulch

Reputation with the Silverwing Sentinels can be earned by achieving victory in the Warsong Gulch battleground, as well as capturing flags during the battleground.

Warsong Scramble, added in 7.2, is one of the rotating PvP brawls. Participating in this brawl is the most efficient way to achieve reputation.

  • Warsong Gulch rewards you with potential reputation. ( more wins needed)
  • Warsong Scramble rewards you with potential reputation. ( more wins needed)
