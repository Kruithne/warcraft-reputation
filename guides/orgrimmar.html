Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Orgrimmar:

  • Durotar
  • Azshara
  • Blasted Lands
  • The Barrens
  • Silverpine Forest
  • Twilight Highlands

Dungeons

The Orgrimmar Tabard can be purchased from Stone Guard Nargol in Orgrimmar which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Orgrimmar.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Orgrimmar Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Orgrimmar using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
