Neutral to Exalted

Breaching the Tomb

Completing the Legionfall campaign quests needed for the Breaching the Tomb achievement reward reputation with the Armies of Legionfall.

World Quests: Broken Shore

The world quests located on the Broken Shore reward reputation with the Armies of Legionfall. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary box available for some extra reputation!

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with the Armies of Legionfall can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding reputation.
