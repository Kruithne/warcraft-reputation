Rewards

Guide

Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Val'sharah

Dreamweavers are the primary faction in Val'sharah. Completing all of the quests in the zone will reward you with roughly 12,000 reputation

World Quests: Val'sharah

Similar to the quests, many of the world quests found in Val'sharah at level 110 or higher award reputation with the Dreamweavers. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary reward available for the Dreamweavers, offering extra reputation.

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with the Dreamweavers can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding 1500 reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding 250 reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding 250 reputation.

Darkheart Thicket

The Darkheart Thicket dungeon rewards players with reputation depending on the difficulty. Completing the dungeon on Mythic+ difficulty is a repeatable method to earn reputation with the faction.
