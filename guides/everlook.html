Hated to Neutral

Southsea Pirates (Recommended)

One of the most popular methods to obtaining reputation with the Steamwheedle Cartel is to kill Southsea Pirates located east of Caverns of Time in Tanaris.

Despite being slower than the alternative method to obtaining neutral, this method does not reduce your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers, allowing you to be exalted with both the Bloodsail Buccaneers and the goblin towns at the same time.

  • Southsea Pirates award reputation per kill.
  • To reach neutral you will need to kill more pirates.

Making Amends

The fastest method to recover your reputation with Everlook is to repeat the Making Amends quest. The repeatable quest involves taking 40x Rune Cloth and 4x Coal to Bronn Fitzwrench located outside Everlook in Winterspring.

This method will reduce your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers. If you wish to maintain your reputation with the Bloodsail Buccaneers, use the alternative method.

  • Making Amends rewards reputation.
  • To reach neutral you will need to make hand-ins. (x Rune Cloth and x Coal)
