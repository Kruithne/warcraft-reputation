Neutral to Exalted

World Quests: Argus

Many of the world quests located in Antoran Wastes, Krokuun and Mac'aree reward reputation with the Army of the Light. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary box available for some extra reputation!

Weekly Quests

Once per week, players can complete the Fuel of a Doomed World and Invasion Onslaught quests available on The Vindicaar.

Reputation Tokens

Account-bound reputation tokens can be earned through various activities such as order hall missions and invasion point bosses.
