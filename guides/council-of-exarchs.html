Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Draenor

Many of the quests from on Draenor reward reputation with the Council of Exarchs. It's recommended to complete the quests in all zones before moving onto farming this reputation.

A number of bonus objectives also reward reputation with the Council of Exarchs, so complete those as you quest for additional reputation.

Construct a level 3 Trading Post in your garrison first for to benefit from the 20% extra reputation Trading Pact provides.

Shadowmoon Valley

Reputation with the Council of Exarchs can be obtained by slaying enemies located in level 100 areas of Shadowmoon Valley: Socrethar's Rise, Pillars of Fate and Darktide Roost.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
