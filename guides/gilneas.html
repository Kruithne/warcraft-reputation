Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Gilneas:

  • Gilneas (Worgen)
  • Duskwood
  • Felwood

Dungeons

The Gilneas Tabard can be purchased from Lord Candren in Darnassus which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Gilneas.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Gilneas Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Gilneas using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
