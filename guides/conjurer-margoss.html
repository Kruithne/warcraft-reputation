Stranger to Best Friend

Drowned Mana

Conjurer Margoss can be found at Margoss Retreat, a lake on a floating island off the north-west side of Dalaran.

While fishing at the lake, you have a chance to catch Drowned Mana, which is used to increase your reputation with Conjurer Margoss as well as buy the items which he sells.

  • Drowned Mana rewards reputation each.

You also have a chance to fish up Mark of Aquaos, an uncommon item which summons Aquaos the Unleashed, who upon death will increase the catch-rate of Drowned Mana to 100% for a short period of time.

It's best to farm this reputation in a group, as more people getting the Mark of Aquaos, the faster it will go!
