Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Darnassus:

  • Teldrassil
  • Darkshore
  • Feralas
  • Ashenvale

Dungeons

The Darnassus Tabard can be purchased from Moon Priestess Lasara in Darnassus which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Darnassus.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Darnassus Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Darnassus using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
