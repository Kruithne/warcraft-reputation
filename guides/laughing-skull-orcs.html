Neutral to Exalted

Gorgrond

To obtain reputation with Laughing Skull Orcs, you need to have a level 2 Trading Post in your garrison. Before starting, it's recommended to upgrade the trading post to level 3 to benefit from the 20% extra reputation Trading Pact provides.

Once your trading post is set-up, reputation can be obtained by slaying enemies located in level 100 areas of Gorgrond; The Pit and the Everbloom Wilds.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
