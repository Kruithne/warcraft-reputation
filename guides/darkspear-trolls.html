Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with the Darkspear Trolls:

  • Durotar
  • Echo Isles
  • Stranglethorn Vale
  • Feralas
  • Zangarmarsh

Dungeons

The Darkspear Tabard can be purchased from Champion Uru'zin in Orgrimmar which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to the Darkspear Trolls.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Sen'jin Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with the Darkspear Trolls using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
