Guide

Neutral to Exalted

Maraudine Prisoner Manifest

To begin with, head on over to Botanist Ferrah located at Karnum's Glade in Desolace.

Complete the three quests which she offers:

  • A Time to Reap - Collect 10 Aloe Thistle.
  • Pulling Weeds - Slay 7 Uprooted Lashers.
  • Taking Part - Plant 5 Cenarion Seeds.

Once you've completed the above three quests, head to Garren Darkwind on the right and follow the next line of quests.

  • An Introduction Is In Order - Speak with Khan Leh'Prah.
  • Breakout! - Free the Kolkar Prisoners.
  • Cenarion Property - Collect 7 Cenarion Supply Crates.
  • Delicate Negotiations - Ride Khan'Prah into battle.
  • Will Work For Food - Collect 5 Basilisk Flank.
  • My Word is My Bond - Speak with Khan Leh'Prah.
  • Three Princes - Defeat Rhoho, Sherik and Jorreth.
  • Not So Fast! - Slay 10 Maraudine Mauler.

While killing the Maraudine Maulers for Not So Fast!, you should hopefully have a Maraudine Prisoner Manifest drop, which allows you to start a quest. If not, just keep killing the Maraudine Maulers until it drops.

Continue following the line of quests from the tablet.

  • Behind Closed Doors - Speak with Kherrah.
  • Time For Change - Speak with Khan Leh'Prah.
  • Into the Fray! - Slay 15 warlocks, 3 doomguards and Than'darissa.
  • Death to Agogridon - Defeat Agogridon.

Upon handing in the last quest, Death to Agogridon, you should now be Exalted with the Magram Clan Centaur and the Gelkis Clan Centaur!

If you had already completed this quest-line, you can talk to Karnum Marshweaver at Karnum's Glade in Desolace to reach Exalted!
