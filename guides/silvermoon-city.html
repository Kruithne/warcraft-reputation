Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Silvermoon City:

  • Eversong Woods
  • Ghostlands
  • Hellfire Peninsula

Dungeons

The Silvermoon City Tabard can be purchased from Magistrix Nizara in Silvermoon City which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Silvermoon City.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Silvermoon Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Silvermoon City using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
