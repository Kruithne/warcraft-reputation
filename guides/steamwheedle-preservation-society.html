Neutral to Exalted

Nagrand Tokens (Recommended)

The fastest and most popular way to obtain reputation with the Steamwheedle Preservation Society is to collect tokens that drop from rare enemies found in Nagrand.

  • Aogexon's Fang from Aogexon rewards reputation.
  • Bergruu's Horn from Bergruu rewards reputation.
  • Dekorhan's Tusk from Dekorhan rewards reputation.
  • Direhoof's Hide from Direhoof rewards reputation.
  • Gagrog's Skull from Gagrog the Brutal rewards reputation.
  • Mu'gra's Head from Mu'gra rewards reputation.
  • Thek'talon's Talon from Thek'talon rewards reputation.
  • Xelganak's Stinger from Xelganak rewards reputation.
  • Vileclaw's Claw from Vileclaw rewards reputation.

To reach Exalted, you'll need to collect more rare tokens.

Garrison Mission: Errand for the SPS

The rare garrison mission Errand for the SPS rewards players with an account-bound Highmaul Relic token when completed successfully.

  • Highmaul Relics award reputation with Steamwheedle Preservation Society.
  • To reach Exalted you'll need more tokens.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
