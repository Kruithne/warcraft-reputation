Guide

Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Highmountain

Highmountain Tribe are the primary faction in Highmountain. Completing all of the quests in the zone will reward you with roughly 16,000 reputation

World Quests: Highmountain

Similar to the quests, many of the world quests found in Highmountain at level 110 or higher award reputation with the Highmountain Tribe. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary reward available for the Highmoutain Tribe, offering extra reputation.

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with the Highmountain Tribe can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding reputation.

Neltharion's Lair

The Neltharion's Lair dungeon rewards players with reputation depending on the difficulty. Completing the dungeon on Mythic+ difficulty is a repeatable method to earn reputation with the faction.
