Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Draenor

Many of the quests from on Draenor reward reputation with the Frostwolf Orcs. It's recommended to complete the quests in all zones before moving onto farming this reputation.

A number of bonus objectives also reward reputation with the Frostwolf Orcs, so complete those as you quest for additional reputation.

Construct a level 3 Trading Post in your garrison first for to benefit from the 20% extra reputation Trading Pact provides.

Frostfire Ridge

Reputation with the Frostwolf Orcs can be obtained by slaying enemies located in level 100 areas of Frostfire Ridge: Stonefury Cliffs, Magnarok and Iron Siegeworks.

Medallion of the Legion

Reputation with all factions from Warlords of Draenor can be increased by using a Medallion of the Legion, increasing your reputation by . To reach Exalted, you'll need more medallions.

Medallion of the Legion can be traded and bought/sold on the auction house.
