Neutral to Exalted

Magister's Terrace (Recommended)

Located at the north east corner of the Isle of Quel'danas, the Magister's Terrace dungeon rewards reputation on both Heroic (once per day) and Normal difficulties, providing all enemies inside are cleared.

  • Heroic rewards reputation per daily run.
  • Normal rewards reputation per run.

If you're clearing Magister's Terrace for the first time, be sure to check the quests listed below for some extra reputation.

Magister's Terrace Quests

As well as offering reputation for every run, the Magister's Terrace dungeon also comes with five quests that can be completed for extra reputation. The first three are obtained from Exarch Larethor at the start of the dungeon, leading into the remaining quests.

  • A Radical Notion rewards reputation.
  • Twisted Associations rewards reputation.
  • Severed Communications rewards reputation.
  • The Scryer's Scryer rewards reputation.
  • Hard to Kill rewards reputation.

Shattered Sun Offensive Dailies

The Isle of Quel'danas provides a wealth of dailies that can be completed for reputation with the Shattered Sun offensive.

  • Further Conversions rewards reputation.
  • Arm the Wards! rewards reputation.
  • The Battle Must Go On rewards reputation.
  • The Air Strikes Must Continue rewards reputation.
  • Maintaining the Sunwell Portal rewards reputation.
  • Know Your Ley Lines rewards reputation.
  • Keeping the Enemy at Bay rewards reputation.
  • Crush the Dawnblade rewards reputation.
  • Disrupt the Greengill Coast rewards reputation.
  • Don't Stop Now.... rewards reputation.
  • Ata'mal Armaments rewards reputation.
  • Rediscovering Your Roots rewards reputation.
  • Open for Business rewards reputation.
  • Your Continued Support rewards reputation.

Completing all of the dailies will earn you reputation every day.
You'll need to do the dailies for more days to reach exalted!
