Rewards

Note: Some of the rewards above require specific achievements to be completed by the guild to unlock.

Guide

Neutral to Exalted

Questing

Completing any quests found in the game while in a guild will grant you reputation toward your guild. The amount of reputation received is based on how much experience the quest would reward.

Dungeons and Raids

Bosses found inside dungeons and raids will reward reputation providing you are in a guild group! A guild group is a party or raid that consists primarily of members from the same guild.

PvP

Winning battlegrounds or arena matches while in a group consisting primarily of members from the same guild will reward you with reputation toward the guild!

Guild Tabards

To help speed up gaining reputation with your guild, there are two tabards you can purchase from a guild vendor.

  • Illustrious Guild Tabard (+50%) can be purchased once you reach Friendly.
  • Renowned Guild Tabard (+100%) can be purchased once you reach Honored.
