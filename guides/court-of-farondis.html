Guide

Neutral to Exalted

Quests: Aszuna

Court of Farondis is the primary faction in Azsuna. Completing all of the quests in the zone will reward you with roughly 12,000 reputation

World Quests: Azsuna

Similar to the quests, many of the world quests found in Azsuna at level 110 or higher award reputation with Court of Farondis. Depending on the day, there may also be an emissary reward available for Court of Farondis, offering extra reputation.

Reputation Tokens

Tokens that increase your reputation with Court of Farondis can be earned through a variety of methods.

  • Kirin Tor emissary rewards have the option of a token rewarding 1500 reputation.
  • Order hall missions may offer account-bound tokens rewarding 250 reputation each.
  • Treasure chests on the Broken Isles have a chance to include tokens rewarding 250 reputation.

Eye of Azshara

The Eye of Azshara dungeon rewards players with reputation depending on the difficulty. Completing the dungeon on Mythic+ difficulty is a repeatable method to earn reputation with the faction.
