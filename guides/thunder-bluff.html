Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Thunder Bluff:

  • Mulgore
  • Southern Barrens
  • Thousand Needles
  • Feralas

Dungeons

The Thunder Bluff Tabard can be purchased from Brave Tuho in Thunder Bluff which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Thunder Bluff.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Thunder Bluff Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Thunder Bluff using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
