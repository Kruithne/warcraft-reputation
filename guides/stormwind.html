Neutral to Exalted

Quests

The following zones have a large quantity of quests that reward reputation with Stormwind:

  • Arathi Highlands
  • Duskwood
  • Dustwallow Marsh
  • Elwynn Forest
  • Redridge Mountains
  • Twilight Highlands
  • Western Plaguelands
  • Westfall

Dungeons

The Stormwind Tabard can be purchased from Captain Lancy Revshon in Stormwind City which applies all reputation gains from dungeons to Stormwind.

Molten Front

Zen'Vorka, one of the vendors located in the Molten Front, allows players to buy a Stormwind Writ of Commendation which rewards reputation.

To reach exalted with Stormwind using this method, you need more commendations.

With each commendation costing 30 Marks of the World tree, you'll need a total of more marks.
