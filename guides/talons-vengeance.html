Neutral to Exalted

Ivory Talon

To first access this reputation, you must complete one of the quest-lines offered by the four falcosaur companion pets.

  • Snowfeather Hatchling
  • Direbeak Hatchling
  • Sharptalon Hatchling
  • Bloodgazer Hatchling

Once you have completed the quest-line offered by one of the above companion pets and obtained the respective mount, you can head Sylvan Falls in Highmountain.

Once there, use your mount in-front of Aviana and follow her dialog options to unlock the Talon's Vengeance faction. Head over to Trinket, a raven sat at the base of the nearby statue, a purchase an Ivory Talon.

Using the Ivory Talon allows you to obtain Marks of Prey when landing killing blows on players located in PvP, Warden Tower or Falcosaur world quest areas on the Broken Isles.

  • Mark of Prey awards reputation.

Some important things to note about using the Ivory Talon:

  • You are hostile to all players - including your own faction.
  • When a player is killed they reward no marks for 10 minutes.
  • The effect does NOT persist through death and has a 15 minute cooldown.
  • You do not gain marks from killing NPCs at the designated areas.
  • NPCs at the Warden Towers will be neutral to you, regardless of faction.
  • While active, you are unable to interact with most NPCs.
  • The buff cannot be manually removed once applied.

Ivory Feather

Once you reach Honored, Trinket will sell you an Ivory Feather that can be used in the same way the Ivory Talon could, however it works in battlegrounds and Ashran rather than world quest areas.
